# Experience reviewer — truth & voice rules

The rules the **QA / improvement** phase checks every artifact against before a packet is
called done. Two axes: is it **true**, and does it sound like **you**. Customize the voice
section so drafts come back in your register, not a generic one.

## Truth rules (hard gates — a failure blocks the packet)
1. **Anchored.** Every resume bullet, evidence-map claim, and cover-letter fact traces to a
   verified entry in `Resume/Experience.md` or the story bank. No orphan claims.
2. **Interview-backtrack test.** For each line: could you explain it in an interview without
   saying "well, what I actually meant was…"? If not, soften or cut it.
3. **Company claims sourced.** Every company-specific statement (products, partnerships,
   numbers, news) is backed by a source in the recruiter brief, or it's generalized/removed.
4. **No inflation.** Reframing emphasis is fine; upgrading scope, level, or metrics is not.
5. **Honored guardrails.** Nothing in the story bank's "do NOT claim" list appears anywhere.

## Voice rules (customize these to you)
- **Register:** e.g. warm-direct, plain, low-adjective. First person, active voice.
- **Signature moves I like:** e.g. lead with the outcome; short sentences; concrete nouns.
- **Words/phrases I never use:** add your personal banned list on top of the house style
  (no em-dashes, no "passionate about", "leverage", "hit the ground running", "synergies").
- **Formatting I prefer:** bullet density, whether I use a summary line, date format, etc.

## Review output
Return a pass/fail line per artifact with the specific offending text quoted, then either
"clear to assemble" or a short fix list. Anything learned that would help *next* role goes
into `workflow-audit/improvements.md`.
