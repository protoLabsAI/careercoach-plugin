# Your Career Coach workspace

This folder is your job-search workspace. The Career Coach's **role-packet** skill files a
folder per role you pursue and fills it with tailored artifacts, one gated step at a time.

```
Companies/<Company>/Roles/<Role - Req>/   ← one folder per role (created as you go)
  job description (raw).md                 the posting, unedited
  process_log.md                           what happened, when
  recruiter brief.md                       company + screener research
  hiring manager profile.md                (only if a credible manager is found)
  role evidence map.md                     each JD requirement → your proof / gap
  tailored resume.md                       reframed for this role, never fabricated
  application skills entry list.md         the discrete skills the ATS form wants
  cover letter.md                          forward-looking, task-solving
  role packet.md                           the assembled deliverable
  orchestration log.md                     completeness checklist

Resume/Experience.md          ← YOUR source of truth. Fill this in first. Verified only.
Agent/story-bank.md           ← reusable proof stories + guardrails
Agent/experience-reviewer.md  ← the truth + voice rules the QA step enforces
Skills/Humanize/SKILL.md      ← writing-quality guidance
workflow-audit/improvements.md ← reusable process fixes learned across roles
```

## Start here

1. **Fill in `Resume/Experience.md`** — your verified roles, metrics, education, and links.
   Everything the coach writes is anchored to this file. If it isn't in here (and true), it
   doesn't go on a resume.
2. **Seed `Agent/story-bank.md`** with a handful of your strongest proof stories.
3. Then paste a posting to your coach and say **"run the role packet"** — it walks the
   phases with you, pausing for your approval at each one.

Nothing here is auto-overwritten: re-seeding the workspace only adds missing files, it never
clobbers your edits.
