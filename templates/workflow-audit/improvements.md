# Workflow audit — reusable improvements

The QA phase writes here whenever a role surfaces a **process** fix worth keeping — something
that would make the *next* packet better, not just this one. Over time this becomes your
personal playbook. Review it before starting a new role; promote recurring items into your
`experience-reviewer.md` or story bank.

## Format
Append newest first. Keep each entry to a few lines: what went wrong or could be better, the
fix, and where it applies.

---

### <date> — <role that surfaced it>
- **Observed:** what slowed us down / read weak / had to be redone.
- **Fix:** the concrete change to make next time.
- **Applies to:** which phase (intake / recruiter / resume / skills / cover / QA) or reference file.
